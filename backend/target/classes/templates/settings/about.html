Project Scope & Objective

The Smart Old Age Home Management System is designed to streamline day-to-day operations for residential nursing homes, caring foundations and senior citizens welfare centers. Key capabilities include residents registrations, donations tracking, medical prescriptions audits, inventory items counts and analytics report generators.

Created as a contribution to the GTU Societal Internship initiatives to digitalize care institutions and automate resource allocations.
